About SmartClean AI

SmartClean AI is an iOS application designed to help users analyze storage usage, remove unnecessary files, and keep their device organized efficiently. The app provides smart cleaning suggestions and home screen widgets to monitor storage, battery, and cleanup status in real time.

Main Features

  • Analyze storage usage and large files
  • Smart cleanup suggestions
  • Duplicate and unnecessary file detection
  • Home screen widgets for storage, battery, and cleanup

Frequently Asked Questions

Does SmartClean AI delete files automatically?
No. The app only suggests files. Users always confirm before deletion.

Does SmartClean AI collect personal data?
No personal files are uploaded. All analysis happens locally on your device.
